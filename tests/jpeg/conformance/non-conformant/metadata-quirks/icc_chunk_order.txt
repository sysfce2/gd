File: icc_chunk_order.jpeg
Source: jpeg-decoder test suite

Non-conformance: ICC profile chunks appear out of sequence order in the file.

Strict behavior: MAY reject - chunks not in ascending order.
Lenient behavior: SHOULD reorder chunks and decode ICC profile.

ICC chunks should appear in order (chunk 1, chunk 2, etc.) but the
spec doesn't strictly require it. Decoders should handle reordering.
