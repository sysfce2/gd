Source: Brother DSmobile 920DW scanner, provided by @filipl

Non-conformance: Extra bytes present after entropy-coded scan data,
before or instead of proper EOI marker.

Strict behavior: MAY reject - unexpected data after scan.
Lenient behavior: SHOULD decode - ignore trailing garbage.

This is a common issue with some scanner firmware and image editors.
Most production decoders (libjpeg, browsers) handle this gracefully.
