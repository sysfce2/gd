Non-conformance: Multiple 0xFF bytes appear before EOI marker.
In JPEG, 0xFF followed by 0x00 is a stuffed byte (escaped 0xFF in data),
but 0xFF followed by another 0xFF is technically a fill byte.

Strict behavior: MAY reject - unusual marker sequence.
Lenient behavior: SHOULD decode - skip fill bytes, find EOI.

The JPEG spec allows fill bytes (0xFF padding) between markers,
but multiple consecutive 0xFF bytes before EOI is unusual.
